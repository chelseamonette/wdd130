Reno-Sparks, Nevada, USA

Reno Sparks is a set of two cities that are next to each other and are known as the greater Northern Nevada area. They are known for their gambling and also their close proximity to both the Sierra Nevada Mountains and the world renowned Lake Tahoe. Northern Nevada has all four seasons and its residents enjoy many outdoor activities that include: hiking, skiing, snowboarding, boating, fishing, hunting, rock climbing, and many others.
